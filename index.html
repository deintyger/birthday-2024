I’m grateful for how far I've come, for being here to witness God’s love, and for the chance to keep fulfilling His purpose in my life.

That’s what truly makes me happy. I’m proud of the woman I’m becoming every day. You know, most people might say, “If you’d told me a year ago I’d be doing this, I wouldn’t have believed it.” Well, I’m not most people—I always knew, haha!
